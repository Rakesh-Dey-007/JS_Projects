Rust : The Rising Threat in Cyberattacks

Rust, a modern programming language, is becoming the weapon of choice for cyberattackers. Ransomware and malware families like BlackCat, Hive, and RansomExx are leveraging Rust to evade traditional threat detection and complicate reverse engineering. An estimated 10-15% of ransomware attacks are now coded in Rust, highlighting the increasing sophistication and adaptability of cyber threats.
