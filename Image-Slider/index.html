vrindaban prem mandir
Prem Mandir in Vrindavan symbolizes divine love in Sanatan Dharma. Dedicated to Radha-Krishna, its intricate marble architecture depicts their life. A revered pilgrimage site for devotees seeking spiritual connection.
Sun Rise
In Sanatan Dharma, sunrise symbolizes renewal and divine light. As Surya, the Sun God, ascends, the sky glows with golden hues, representing hope and the triumph of light over darkness.
Kedarnath Mandir
Kedarnath Temple, a Jyotirlinga, is a revered pilgrimage site known for spiritual purification and blessings from Lord Shiva. It holds historical significance, nestled in the Himalayas amidst breathtaking natural beauty.
Mayapur Mandir
Mayapur Mandir: Birthplace of Chaitanya Mahaprabhu, ISKCON headquarters, famous for the Temple of the Vedic Planetarium and Gaura Purnima festival.
Ayodha Ram Mandir
Ayodhya Ram Mandir: Sacred Hindu temple under construction at Lord Ram's birthplace. Symbolizes Hindu identity, built on site of former Babri Masjid. Holds immense cultural and religious significance for Hindus worldwide.
Rishikesh ganga aarti
In Sanatan Dharma, Rishikesh Ganga Aarti is a revered daily ritual on the Ganges banks in Rishikesh, Uttarakhand. It involves prayers, hymns, and fire rituals, symbolizing devotion to the sacred river.
